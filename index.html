  1. The object of the game is to capture all of your opponent's pieces
  2. Players take turns to move one piece at the time
  3. Pieces can jump over any adiacent piece if space is available
  4. If a jump is made and another jump is available than player continues his moves
  5. Game ends when a player has only one piece left
